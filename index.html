About Us

Under supervision of Dr. Moustafa ElDakak, head of the genetics department, and Dr. Sarah Amgad. We, the genetics department graduates, through our graduation projects, applied academic principles to structured research aimed at exploring the biological world to form bioproducts.

After 2 years reflecting methodological rigor and scientific responsibility, our work reflects readiness to contribute to the scientific and professional genetics community, being honored to belong to this great institution.

What We Do

At the intersection of nature and technology, we conduct cutting-edge scientific experiments to unlock the potential of microorganisms.

Our focus areas include environmental cleanup (bioremediation), the development of 100% biodegradable bioplastics, and the synthesis of carbon-neutral bioenergy alternatives.

By mimicking and accelerating natural processes, we are paving the way for a circular economy that works in harmony with the Earth's ecosystems.

Innovation & Future Direction

We are constantly exploring new frontiers in synthetic biology. By integrating machine learning with bioinformatics, we can predict organism behavior and design highly specific enzymes.

Our future goals include scaling up our photobioreactors for decentralized energy production and engineering fungi that can safely break down complex electronic waste.
